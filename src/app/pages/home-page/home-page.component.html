Types of Motor Insurance
Car Insurance

Car insurance offers comprehensive financial protection for your vehicle against damages caused by accidents, theft, fire, or natural disasters. It ensures compliance with legal requirements by covering third-party liabilities. With optional add-ons like zero depreciation and roadside assistance, car insurance enhances your safety and peace of mind. It also provides coverage for repair costs, helping you avoid unexpected financial burdens.

Bike Insurance

Bike insurance safeguards your two-wheeler against accidental damage, theft, and natural calamities, ensuring uninterrupted mobility. It covers third-party liabilities, keeping you legally compliant while on the road. With affordable premiums and customizable add-ons like engine protection and helmet coverage, bike insurance ensures worry-free rides. It also provides financial assistance for repairs, reducing your out-of-pocket expenses.

Types of Vehicle Insurance We Provide
Third-Party

Third-party insurance is a mandatory policy that protects you from financial liabilities arising from damages or injuries caused to a third party. It covers legal obligations, ensuring compliance with road safety laws. While it does not cover your own vehicle's damages, it provides significant relief from third-party claims. Ideal for cost-conscious vehicle owners, it offers basic but essential coverage for accident-related liabilities.

Comprehensive

Comprehensive insurance provides extensive protection for your vehicle, covering both third-party liabilities and damages to your own vehicle. It includes coverage for accidents, theft, natural calamities, and vandalism, offering peace of mind in unforeseen events. With optional add-ons like zero depreciation, engine protection, and roadside assistance, it enhances your coverage. This policy is ideal for those seeking all-around financial security for their vehicle.

Why Buy Vehicle Insurance?

Vehicle insurance is not just a legal mandate in India under the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, but a vital financial safeguard. With over 4.5 lakh road accidents and 2 lakh vehicle thefts annually, having insurance protects you from significant financial liabilities. Comprehensive policies cover damages caused by natural disasters like floods and earthquakes, which are increasingly common. Repair costs for vehicles can be steep, ranging from ₹5,000 to over ₹1,00,000, making insurance a cost-effective solution. Third-party insurance, which accounts for 70% of motor claims in India, shields you from liabilities involving others. Affordable premiums, starting at just ₹500 for two-wheelers, and customizable add-ons ensure peace of mind while driving. Moreover, tax benefits for self-employed individuals make it an even smarter investment.
